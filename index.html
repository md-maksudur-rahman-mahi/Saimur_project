About NordWind Solutions

Providing Sustainable Urban Energy Solutions

NordWind Solutions develops and sells omnidirectional wind turbine systems to buildings in Lappeenranta. We help buildings produce their own electricity locally through small wind turbines mounted on rooftops or suitable facades.

Our comprehensive solution package includes site analysis, system sales, installation, smart energy monitoring, maintenance, and future upgrades.

Our Technology

Advanced Urban Wind Solutions

Our omnidirectional wind turbine systems are designed specifically for urban environments where wind direction can be variable and buildings can accommodate distributed generation technologies.

Key Features:

  • Omnidirectional design - captures wind from any direction
  • Site-specific assessment using CFD analysis
  • Complementary to solar in high-latitude climates
  • Low noise and vibration design
  • Building-integrated aesthetic

Technical Approach:

We use Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) to analyze urban wind patterns, turbulence, and site-specific conditions to ensure optimal turbine placement and performance.
